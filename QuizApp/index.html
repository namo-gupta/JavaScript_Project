The Quiz

  • This quiz app exclusively consists of multiple-choice questions, where each question has one correct answer among the provided options.
  • Each question is allotted a time limit of 30 seconds. Users must select their answer within this time frame.
  • Users will receive points based on the accuracy of their answers. Correct answers will earn points, while incorrect answers will not affect the score.
  • Immediate feedback on the correctness of answers will be provided after each question.
  • Any disputes or concerns regarding the quiz or its results can be communicated to our support team for further review.
